## PERSONALIZATION TIPS

**Always replace before sending:**
- `[Name]` — their actual first name
- `[Company]` — their company name
- `[space]` — their industry (fintech, logistics, SaaS)
- `[topic]` — their recent LinkedIn post or activity
- `[date]` — your actual availability date

**Check before sending:**
- Have they posted recently? Reference it.
- Do they have open roles on LinkedIn? Acknowledge it.
- Have they shared a tech article? Use it as the opening.

**Response rate benchmark:**
- Cold outreach: 8–15% response rate is good
- Personalized with specific reference: 20–30%
- Warm intro or mutual connection: 40–60%
